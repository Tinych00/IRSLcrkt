About Us

Our Journey

A Legacy of Passion and Community

Established in the heart of Ingleburn, the Ingleburn RSL Cricket Club has been a pillar of the local community for decades. With a rich history dating back to the early 1960s, the club was founded by a group of passionate cricket enthusiasts who sought to bring together the community through the love of the game.

Over the years, the club has grown from a small group of players into a thriving organization that now boasts a wide array of teams, ranging from juniors to seniors. Our commitment to nurturing talent and fostering a sense of camaraderie has remained steadfast, making us one of the most respected clubs in the region.

Junior Teams

The Ingleburn RSL Cricket Club takes great pride in its junior teams, which have become the lifeblood of our organization. Our junior programs are designed to introduce young players to the game, instilling in them the core values of teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship. With dedicated coaches and a supportive environment, our junior teams have consistently produced talented players who go on to excel at higher levels.

Senior Teams

Our senior teams represent the pinnacle of cricket at Ingleburn RSL Cricket Club. With a mix of seasoned veterans and up-and-coming talent, our senior teams compete in various local and regional leagues. The camaraderie and competitive spirit within our senior squads are second to none, and they have brought home numerous accolades over the years.

Our Future

As we look to the future, the Ingleburn RSL Cricket Club remains committed to its founding principles of community, passion, and excellence. We continue to invest in our facilities, coaching staff, and player development programs to ensure that the next generation of cricketers has the best possible platform to succeed. We invite you to join us on this journey and be a part of our proud history.
